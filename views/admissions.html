Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to secure your place at Adarsha Secondary School

01

Application

  • Complete application form accurately
  • Submit all required documents
  • Pay application fee as specified
  • Provide previous academic records
  • Submit recent passport-size photographs
02

Evaluation

  • Entrance examination (if applicable)
  • Thorough document verification
  • Personal interview for senior levels
  • Merit-based selection process
  • Academic record assessment
03

Admission

  • Receive admission offer letter
  • Complete fee payment process
  • Submit original documents
  • Attend orientation program
  • Begin your academic journey

Important Dates

Mark your calendar with these crucial admission timeline dates

School Programs

Grades PlayGroup - 10

  • Application Opens: Falgun (February)
  • Entrance Examination: Chaitra (March)
  • Results Published: Early Baisakh (April)
  • Classes Begin: Baisakh (April/May)
  • Late admission: Till Jestha

+2 Programs

Higher Secondary Education

  • Application: Post SEE Results
  • Entrance Exam: Within one week
  • Merit List: Within 3 days
  • Classes Begin: Shrawan (July)
  • Document verification: Ongoing

Technical Programs

CTEVT Diploma Courses

  • Application: Post SEE Results
  • CTEVT Entrance: As per board
  • Selection Process: Merit-based
  • Classes Begin: Shrawan (July)
  • Limited seats available

Eligibility Criteria

Ensure you meet the requirements for your desired program before applying

School Level

PlayGroup to Grade 10

  • Age-appropriate for respective class
  • Previous class marksheet/certificate
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • Guardian consent form

+2 Programs

Science, Management, Education

  • SEE passed or equivalent qualification
  • Minimum GPA requirements met
  • Original SEE marksheet & certificate
  • Character certificate from previous school
  • Migration certificate (if required)
  • Subject-specific prerequisites

Technical Programs

CTEVT Diploma Courses

  • SEE with required core subjects
  • CTEVT entrance qualification
  • Technical aptitude assessment
  • All required documents verified
  • Medical fitness (for some programs)
  • Age limit as per CTEVT norms
