Our History

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1930

1930 - Foundation

Adarsha Secondary School was established in 1930 AD (1986 BS) as a community-based educational institution in Biratnagar, Morang. The school was founded with the vision of providing quality education to the local community.

1950s - Growth and Development

The school experienced significant growth during this period, expanding its infrastructure and student capacity. This decade marked the beginning of systematic academic programs and the introduction of modern teaching methodologies.

1970s - Academic Excellence

Adarsha Secondary School established itself as one of the premier educational institutions in the region, producing numerous distinguished alumni who went on to become successful industrialists, politicians, administrators, and literary figures.

1990s - Modernization

The school underwent significant modernization with the introduction of computer education and science laboratories. This period saw the expansion of academic programs and improvement in educational facilities.

2000s - Technical Education

Introduction of technical education programs including Computer Engineering and Civil Engineering courses affiliated with CTEVT, marking a new chapter in the school's history.

Present Day

Today, Adarsha Secondary School stands as a comprehensive educational institution offering programs from early childhood to higher secondary education, along with technical courses. With over 2,600 students, the school continues its tradition of academic excellence while embracing modern educational practices.

Key Achievements

  • Consistently high performance in national-level examinations
  • Successful implementation of technical education programs
  • Development of modern infrastructure and facilities
  • Strong alumni network in various professional fields
  • Recognition as one of the leading educational institutions in Province 1

Our Legacy

For over nine decades, Adarsha Secondary School has been at the forefront of education in Nepal's eastern region. As the first and finest educational institution in this area, we have illuminated countless lives through quality education. Our commitment to academic excellence, coupled with our focus on character development, has produced generations of leaders who continue to contribute significantly to our nation's progress.
