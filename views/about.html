Our Story

A legacy of educational excellence spanning nearly a century

Established in 1930 AD (1986 BS), Adarsha Secondary School Morang (आदर्श माध्यमिक विद्यालय, विराटनगर, मोरङ्ग) stands as a prestigious community-based educational institution in Biratnagar, Morang, Nepal. As the first and finest educational institution in this region, we have been steadfastly committed to illuminating lives through quality education for generations.
Our rich heritage spans over nine decades, during which we have proudly nurtured and produced numerous distinguished professionals who have made significant contributions to our nation's development. From expert industrialists and visionary politicians to dedicated administrators and celebrated literary figures, our graduates continue to shape Nepal's future across various sectors.

Academic Excellence

Committed to providing world-class education with national recognition

We are proudly affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and fully approved by the Ministry of Education, Government of Nepal. Our comprehensive educational framework encompasses a wide spectrum of academic programs, from foundational PlayGroup education through Grade 10, complemented by specialized Plus Two programs across three distinctive streams: Management, Education, and Science.
Our commitment to academic excellence is reinforced by our expert leadership team, continuous investment in modern infrastructure, and unwavering dedication to maintaining the highest educational standards. We combine traditional values with contemporary teaching methodologies to create an optimal learning environment for our students.

Our Commitment

Dedicated to making quality education accessible and affordable for all

At Adarsha Secondary School, we believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to quality education. We maintain moderate and reasonable fee structures while offering comprehensive scholarship schemes for financially disadvantaged and academically deserving students. Our commitment extends beyond affordability to encompass excellence in every aspect of education.
Our dedication to quality education is strengthened by our expert leadership team, continuous professional development programs for faculty, and ongoing investments in both physical infrastructure and educational resources. We consistently strive to enhance our educational standards while maintaining our core values and community-centered approach.
Through our holistic approach to education, we prepare students not just for academic success, but for meaningful contributions to society. Our graduates carry forward the values and knowledge gained at Adarsha, becoming leaders and change-makers in their respective fields while remaining connected to their community roots.
