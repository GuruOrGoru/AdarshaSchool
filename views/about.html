Our Story

Established in 1930 AD (1986 BS), Adarsha Secondary School Morang (आदर्श माध्यमिक विद्यालय, विराटनगर, मोरङ्ग) is a prestigious community-based educational institution located in Biratnagar, Morang, Nepal.

Our Legacy

As the first and finest educational institution in this area, Adarsha School Biratnagar has been illuminating lives through education for generations. We have proudly produced numerous distinguished professionals including expert industrialists, politicians, administrators, and literary figures who contribute significantly to our nation's development.

Academic Excellence

We are affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. Our comprehensive educational programs range from PlayGroup to Grade 10, complemented by plus two programs in Management, Education, and Science streams.

Technical Education

We also offer specialized technical programs including:

  • Junior Computer Technician (15 months program)
  • Diploma in Computer Engineering (CTEVT affiliated)
  • Diploma in Civil Engineering

Our Commitment

At Adarsha Secondary School, we maintain moderate fee structures and provide scholarship schemes for financially disadvantaged and deserving students. Our commitment to quality education is reinforced by our expert leadership and continuous improvement in both physical infrastructure and educational standards.
