Science Laboratories

Modern, well-equipped laboratories for hands-on learning and scientific exploration

Physics Laboratory

Our physics laboratory is equipped with modern apparatus for conducting experiments in mechanics, electricity, optics, and thermodynamics. Students can explore fundamental physics concepts through hands-on experimentation.

  • Optics and light experiments
  • Mechanics and motion studies
  • Electricity and magnetism apparatus
  • Digital measuring instruments
  • Safety equipment and protocols

Chemistry Laboratory

The chemistry laboratory provides a safe environment for students to conduct chemical experiments, analyze substances, and understand chemical reactions. All safety protocols are strictly followed.

  • Chemical analysis equipment
  • Fume hoods and safety stations
  • Digital balances and pH meters
  • Chemical storage and disposal systems
  • Emergency safety equipment

Biology Laboratory

Our biology laboratory is designed for studying living organisms, conducting dissections, and exploring biological processes. It includes microscopes, specimens, and all necessary equipment for comprehensive biological studies.

  • Compound and stereo microscopes
  • Biological specimens and models
  • Dissection equipment and tools
  • Environmental monitoring devices
  • Digital imaging systems

Library & Computer Lab

Learning resources and digital infrastructure for comprehensive education

Central Library

Our main library houses an extensive collection of books, reference materials, and digital resources. It provides a quiet, comfortable environment for study and research.

  • Over 10,000 books and reference materials
  • Digital library and e-books
  • Quiet study areas and reading corners
  • Computer workstations for research
  • Librarian assistance and guidance

Computer Laboratory

Our computer laboratory is equipped with modern computers and software to support digital learning, programming, and research activities. High-speed internet connectivity ensures seamless access to online resources.

  • 40+ modern desktop computers
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Educational software and applications
  • Programming and coding tools
  • Multimedia and presentation equipment

Sports Complex
